# Examples

- Beg so much his hands look like cups.  — Persistence bordering on need; social read of posture (metaphor).
- She was the feeling before a smile.  — Anticipation embodied; tenderness as prelude (metaphor).
- My dog knows the difference between being tripped over and being kicked.  — Intent matters more than outcome; ethical distinction.
- You can't get somebody from Beaumont that drunk to jump off a porch.  — Risk threshold and refusal under danger; cultural inference.
- Things dats undastood don't need no splaining.  — Shared context reduces the need for explanation; vernacular tone.
